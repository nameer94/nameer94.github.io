MyMAL Privacy Policy

By using MyMAL the app may store data from you, please read the following before using the app.

What data is collected from me?

The app uses your myanimelist.net account to deliver the content for you.

The username and password is used to login to your account, the app uses WebView (something like a browser in the app) to login through myanimelist.net main login page to deliver more secure way of storing your data.

Why I need to share my data with the app?

The data is used to improve the experiant of MyMAL.

  • Manage your anime or manga list.
  • Find what you looking for, and many of myanimelist.net services..

The data is stored in a secure way and will never be shared or sold to any third party.

For more information about myanimelist.net privacy policy please read: myanimelist.net privacy policy

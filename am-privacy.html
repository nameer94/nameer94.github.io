AnimeMedia Privacy Policy

By using AnimeMedia the app may store data from you, please read the following before using the app.

What data is collected from me?

The app uses your myanimelist.net account to deliver the content for you.

The username is used to get your anime lists.

Why I need to share my data with the app?

The data is used to improve the experiant of AnimeMedia.

  • Get your anime lists to search for your favorite anime songs and AMV's.

The data is stored in a secure way and will never be shared or sold to any third party.

For more information about myanimelist.net privacy policy please read: myanimelist.net privacy policy
